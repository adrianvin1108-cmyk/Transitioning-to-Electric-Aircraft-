Summary Problem Plan Benefits Conclusion

Executive Summary

Air travel is one of the most widely used forms of transportation, but it contributes significantly to emissions. This proposal suggests short-term solutions like flight optimization and sustainable fuels, along with a long-term shift to electric aircraft.

The Problem

Aircraft emissions, including CO2 and contrails, contribute heavily to climate change. As air travel continues to grow, so does its environmental impact.

Proposed Plan

Stage 1: Flight Optimization

Adjust routes to reduce contrails.

Stage 2: Sustainable Aviation Fuel

Use SAF to lower emissions.

Stage 3: Electric Aircraft

Develop fully electric commercial planes.

Benefits

Lower Emissions

Lower Costs

Job Growth

Better Image

Solution Benefit
Flight Optimization Quick emission reduction
SAF Cleaner fuel
Electric Aircraft No in-flight emissions

Conclusion

Investment in electric aircraft is necessary to reduce emissions and create a more sustainable future for aviation.
