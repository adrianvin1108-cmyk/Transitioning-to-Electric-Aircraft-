This website presents a proposal for reducing aviation emissions through flight optimization, sustainable aviation fuel adoption, and long-term investment in fully electric aircraft.
Summary Problem Plan Benefits Action

Executive Summary

Air travel is widely used but contributes heavily to emissions. This proposal presents short-term solutions like flight optimization and sustainable fuels, and a long-term solution of electric aircraft.

The Problem

Aircraft emissions, especially contrails and CO2, significantly impact climate change. As air travel grows, this problem becomes more urgent.

Proposed Plan

Stage 1: Flight Optimization

Adjust flight routes to reduce contrails.

Stage 2: Sustainable Aviation Fuel

Use cleaner fuels to reduce emissions short-term.

Stage 3: Electric Aircraft

Develop fully electric commercial planes long-term.

Benefits

Lower Emissions

Near-zero emissions during flight.

Lower Costs

Cheaper fuel and maintenance.

Job Growth

New engineering and tech jobs.

Better Image

Improves airline reputation.
