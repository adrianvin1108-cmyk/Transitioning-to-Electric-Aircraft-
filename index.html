Transitioning to Electric Aircraft

A Proposal to Reduce Aviation Emissions

Prepared for Dr. Matthew Feltman
Penn State Abington

Adrian Vintila & Theodore Fisher
April 26, 2026

A plan to reduce aviation emissions using better routing, cleaner fuels, and electric aircraft.
Summary Problem Plan Benefits Conclusion

Executive Summary

Air travel is efficient but creates large emissions. This proposal suggests short-term fixes like flight optimization and SAF, and a long-term shift to electric aircraft.

The Problem

Aircraft emissions contribute to climate change, especially at high altitudes. As air travel grows, this becomes a bigger issue.

Proposed Plan

Stage 1: Flight Optimization

Change routes to reduce contrails.

Stage 2: Sustainable Fuel

Use SAF to lower emissions.

Stage 3: Electric Aircraft

Develop fully electric commercial planes.

Benefits

Lower Emissions

Lower Costs

More Jobs

Better Image

Conclusion

Investment in electric aircraft is necessary to reduce emissions and create a sustainable future.
